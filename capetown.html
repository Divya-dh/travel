About Cape Town

Cape Town, nestled at the foot of Table Mountain, is a vibrant city renowned for its stunning natural beauty, rich history, and diverse culture. From the iconic Table Mountain and the vibrant V&A Waterfront to the historic Robben Island and the beautiful Cape Peninsula, Cape Town offers a unique blend of adventure, culture, and relaxation.

Suggested Travel Itineraries

3-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Table Mountain, Robben Island

Day 2: Kirstenbosch National Botanical Garden, Camps Bay Beach

Day 3: Cape Point, Chapman's Peak Drive

1-Week Itinerary

Day 1-2: Table Mountain, Robben Island, V&A Waterfront

Day 3-4: Cape Point, Chapman's Peak Drive, Kirstenbosch National Botanical Garden

Day 5-6: Stellenbosch Wine Region, Franschhoek

Day 7: Hiking in Table Mountain National Park
