About Rome

Rome, the capital city of Italy, is a place where history and modern life converge. Known for its ancient monuments, rich culture, and vibrant street life, Rome offers travelers an unforgettable experience full of art, food, and history.

Suggested Travel Itineraries

3-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Colosseum, Roman Forum

Day 2: Vatican Museums, St. Peter's Basilica

Day 3: Pantheon, Trevi Fountain, Piazza Navona

1-Week Itinerary

Day 1-2: Colosseum, Roman Forum, Palatine Hill

Day 3-4: Vatican City, Castel Sant'Angelo, Piazza del Popolo

Day 5-7: Pantheon, Trevi Fountain, Spanish Steps, Tivoli Gardens
