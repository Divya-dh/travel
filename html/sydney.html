About Sydney

Sydney, Australia's largest city, is a vibrant metropolis known for its iconic landmarks, stunning harbour, and beautiful beaches. From the majestic Sydney Opera House and Harbour Bridge to the golden sands of Bondi Beach and the lush Royal Botanic Garden, Sydney offers a wealth of attractions for visitors.

Suggested Travel Itineraries

3-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Sydney Opera House, Harbour Bridge Climb

Day 2: Bondi Beach, Royal Botanic Garden

Day 3: The Rocks, Darling Harbour

1-Week Itinerary

Day 1-2: Sydney Opera House, Harbour Bridge Climb, Taronga Zoo

Day 3-4: Bondi Beach, Coogee Beach, Manly Beach

Day 5: Royal Botanic Garden, The Rocks

Day 6-7: Darling Harbour, Sydney Aquarium, Wild Life Sydney Zoo
