About Bali

Bali, often referred to as the "Island of the Gods," is a captivating destination known for its stunning natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and vibrant arts scene. From pristine beaches and lush rice paddies to ancient temples and bustling markets, Bali offers a diverse and unforgettable experience.

Suggested Travel Itineraries

3-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Ubud Monkey Forest, Tegallalang Rice Terraces

Day 2: Ubud Palace, Ubud Market

Day 3: Tegenungan Waterfall, Tanah Lot Temple

1-Week Itinerary

Day 1-2: Ubud Monkey Forest, Tegallalang Rice Terraces, Ubud Palace

Day 3-4: Nusa Dua Beach, Water sports, Uluwatu Temple

Day 5-6: Lovina Beach, Dolphin Watching, Sekumpul Waterfall

Day 7: Relaxing Spa Day, Sunset at Jimbaran Beach
